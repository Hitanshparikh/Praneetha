Who We Are?

  • Praneetha Ventures, established in 2020, is a dynamic private limited company specializing in consulting services for the trading industry

  • With a strong foundation in this sector, we have built a reputation for delivering expert guidance and strategic insights to our clients


  • As we look toward the future, Praneetha Ventures is actively diversifying our portfolio by expanding into new business areas


  • Our current focus includes entering the green manufacturing sector, where we aim to contribute to innovative and sustainable solutions to existing problems

  • This strategic expansion reflects our commitment to innovation and the evolving needs of our clients and the industries we serve
